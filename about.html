Our Story

Started by Students, Built for Students

InternPath was born in 2022 from a simple frustration: finding the right internship as a college student in India was unnecessarily difficult. Job boards were cluttered with irrelevant listings, scam postings were rampant, and there was no easy way to filter opportunities by major, location, or stipend.

Our founders, a group of IIT and NIT alumni, set out to build the platform they wished existed during their college years. What started as a weekend project has grown into India's most trusted internship aggregator, connecting over 50,000 students with 3,500+ companies across 120 cities.

Today, InternPath is the go-to platform for students looking to kickstart their careers. We verify every company listing, ensure fair stipends, and provide tools that make the application process seamless. For companies, we offer access to a curated pool of talented students from top universities.

What Drives Us

Our Mission and Values

Equal Access

Every student, regardless of their city or university tier, deserves access to quality internship opportunities. Our platform is free for all students, always.

Trust and Safety

Every listing is manually verified. We have zero tolerance for scam postings, unpaid exploitative work, or misleading job descriptions. Your safety is our priority.

Bridge the Gap

We believe practical experience should complement classroom learning. Internships bridge the gap between education and industry, preparing students for successful careers.

Community First

We are building more than a job board. We are building a community of students, mentors, and employers who believe in investing in the next generation of talent.

Our Journey

From Idea to Impact

January 2022

The Beginning

InternPath founded by Rahul and Neha during a weekend hackathon. The first version was a simple spreadsheet shared with 50 students.

June 2022

First 1,000 Users

Launched the website with 200 internship listings from 50 companies. Crossed 1,000 registered students within the first month.

February 2023

Seed Funding

Raised Rs. 5 Crore in seed funding. Expanded the team to 25 members. Launched the employer dashboard and advanced filters.

October 2023

10,000 Students Placed

Milestone: 10,000 students successfully placed through InternPath. Partnered with 1,000+ companies across 50 cities.

August 2024

Series A

Raised Rs. 25 Crore in Series A. Launched university partnerships program connecting with 500+ colleges across India.

2026

50,000 and Growing

Today, 50,000+ students placed, 3,500+ companies onboarded, and presence in 120+ cities. Building the future of campus hiring in India.
